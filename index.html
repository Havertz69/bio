About Me

I'm William Kariuki — a finalist BSc. IT student at Murang'a University of Technology, an IT specialist and a self-driven full-stack developer with real-world experience building and shipping digital products.

I specialise in IT support, Cybersecurity and building clean, scalable web and mobile applications — from intuitive frontends in React to robust backends in Node.js and PHP, backed by MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB. Beyond software, I bring hands-on expertise in IT infrastructure: network setup, CCTV and access control installations, and device troubleshooting. I don't just write code — I solve problems end-to-end.

I'm passionate about clean architecture, continuous learning, and giving back. When I'm not building, I'm mentoring upcoming developers in my community or exploring the intersection of AI and real-world applications. I'm currently open to full-time, internship, and freelance opportunities where I can grow fast and make a real impact.
